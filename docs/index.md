# Welcome to My Digital Lab

Built on the ZeroClaw framework.

## Why I Built This

In the AI era, I don't just want to use tools — I want to build and own intelligent systems.

This lab is where I design, test, and evolve agent-driven workflows that turn thinking into action.

It documents:

- The architecture behind my AI agents
- The workflows that power them
- And importantly — everything that broke along the way

Because the real value isn't just in what works… it's in understanding why it didn't.

---

## Current Lead Agent: Weeny Jeanie — Thought Leadership Companion

My primary agent is an AI Thought Leadership Companion — designed to turn raw, unstructured thinking into high-quality social content.

**What she does:**

- Captures ideas via Telegram (voice, text, image, or links)
- Transforms unstructured input into clear, structured insight
- Generates multiple LinkedIn-ready post variations
- Refines tone, clarity, and positioning based on intent

## Core Capability

Weeny Jeanie acts as a personal thinking partner — not just a content generator.

- Identifies the underlying theme (leadership, AI, creativity, etc.)
- Understands audience and intent
- Shapes ideas into credible, human, insight-led content
- Filters and improves thinking before it becomes output

## Design Philosophy

| Principle | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Human-in-the-loop** | Quality over automation |
| **Insight-first** | Not just content — thinking |
| **Modular** | Designed to evolve into multi-agent systems |
| **Low-friction** | Capture ideas instantly, anywhere |

---

## What This Is (and Isn't)

**This is:**

- A thinking engine
- A content refinement system
- A foundation for future agent collaboration

**This is not (yet):**

- Fully autonomous posting
- Multi-platform optimisation
- Long-form content generation
